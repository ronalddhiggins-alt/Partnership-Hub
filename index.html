As engagement algorithms push users toward outrage and corporate interests clash with community values, Alex and his diverse team face an impossible question: Can technology serve the people who use it, or will profit always win?

From tense board meetings to late-night coding sessions, from personal crises to collective action — a novel about democratic governance, community organizing, and the battles we don't choose but can't afford to lose.
